PRECIPURETM RAINWATER FILTRATION SYSTEMS

PRECIPURETM is one of its kind in-house developed rainwater filtration system with the primary objective of bringing affordability and convenience in rainwater harvesting. It is unique due to its simple user-friendly design and can be used in groundwater recharge as well as rainwater storage systems.

  • Contributes to significant cost reduction by being a replacement for conventional filtration & de-silting components of a rainwater harvesting system
  • High flow rate due to shearing action of water on the screen makes rainwater harvesting possible even from large roofs
  • No moving parts
  • Filter element is 150-1000 micron SS304 V-Wire screen with unique non-clogging continuous slot design
  • Compact design ensures installation in very less space
  • Can be installed easily & quickly by plumber or semi-skilled labour
  • Adaptable to site conditions - can be installed aboveground well as underground
