IMPLEMENTATION

We undertake planning and implementation of all kinds of rainwater harvesting systems ranging from economy to premium and micro to macro level. The projects are undertaken on turnkey basis or as part of the work depending on customer requirements and other factors. Project execution is done keeping in mind high standards of work such as:

  • Good quality workmanship
  • Best quality materials with right dimensions
  • Adherence to relevant standards
  • Detailed & transparent costing
  • Timely completion of project


PRECIPURE® Rainwater Recharge System


  • New-Age modular recharge system
  • Easy and quick to install
  • Requires very less space
  • No deep excavation required
  • Leakage-free system
  • Filter element is 700-1000 micron SS304 V-Wire screen with unique inwardly widening horizontal and continuous slot design to improve and sustain recharge process
  • Filter media is graded and washed 800-1000 micron silica sand for efficient filtration
  • Easy, infrequent and low cost maintenance – can be conveniently done by user
  • Can be provided with first flush system to reduce silt load on filter media thus improving overall efficiency
  • Can be provided with overflow provision to eject excess water during heavy rains
  • Provision of vent pipe for air to escape during recharge process
  • Economy & Premium options available as per requirement



PRECIPURE® Pre-Storage Rainwater Filters


Wall-Mountable Rainwater Filter

  • Compact design ensures installation in very less space
  • Filter element is 150 & 600 micron SS304/LCG removable V-Wire screen with unique non-clogging continuous slot design
  • Filter media is 300-500 micron graded & washed silica sand free of organic impurities which adds extra filtration efficiency as compared to normal filters
  • Coupled with a first flush system to effectively remove physical impurities from rainwater so as to reduce silt load on filter element and media, thus improving its efficiency
  • Can be installed easily & quickly by plumber
  • Easy, less frequent and no cost maintenance – can be conveniently done by user
  • Can be disassembled and reassembled, if required, to change location or for maintenance
  • Variants available as per customer’s specific requirement and budget

  • Compact design ensures installation in very less space
  • Adaptable to site conditions - can be installed aboveground as well as underground
