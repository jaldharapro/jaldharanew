CHAMBULES

Understanding the practical problems faced by plumbers, building owners and builders in arranging expert masons for construction of chambers for various water lines in the building, we have developed this product. These RCC modules have unique features which impart benefits of significant savings in time and labour.

  • Ready-to-install RCC chamber modules. Can effectively replace brick mortar chambers.
  • Can also be used to make trenches and small size tanks.
  • Savings in time and labour costs.
  • Sturdy construction.
  • Modules can be joined horizontally as well as vertically with interlocking joints to achieve desired dimensions of the chamber.
  • Provision of inlet and outlet pipe connections in modules ensures no damage to walls while making pipe connections which happens to be the case in conventional chambers. This also removes need of simultaneous laying of pipeline. Chamber installation can be done independently.
  • Due to smooth finish, plaster on the walls is not required.
  • Can be covered by regular size manhole covers available in the market.
  • Ready mix mortar for filling joints can also be provided alongside to further reduce material and carriage costs.
  • Can be installed above ground also for other applications.
